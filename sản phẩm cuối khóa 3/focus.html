Intelligent Mobility

We develop autonomous, electric, and AI-driven transport systems to create a future of safe, clean, and efficient mobility. From smart traffic solutions to next-gen vehicles, we connect innovation with sustainability.

Sustainable Energy

Our focus lies in transforming energy systems through research in renewables like wind, solar, and hydrogen. We aim to optimize energy storage, grid efficiency, and accelerate the global transition to clean energy.

Maritime Systems

With advanced research in shipbuilding, automation, and ocean engineering, we partner with global maritime industries to innovate sustainable and autonomous marine technologies.

Digital Transformation

Our interdisciplinary teams lead in AI, data science, and cybersecurity to ensure that digital innovation supports ethical, inclusive, and secure progress in all sectors of society.

Sustainable Value Creation

We empower businesses and industries with circular economy principles, responsible production models, and innovation strategies that respect both people and planet.

Social Participation

Through social science and policy-driven research, we ensure every voice is heard. Our focus on inclusion, equity, and democratic participation helps shape fair and resilient communities.

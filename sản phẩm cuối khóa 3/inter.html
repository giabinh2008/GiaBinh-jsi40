Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to speak German?

No, many programs are taught in English. However, learning German is recommended for daily life.

Can I work while studying?

Yes, international students can work up to 120 full days or 240 half days per year.

Is Kiel an expensive city?

Kiel is more affordable than larger German cities, with reasonable rent and living costs.
